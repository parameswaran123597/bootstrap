Swiggy is a leading on-demand convenience platform in India, best known for its online food ordering and delivery service. Founded in 2014, the company partners with a vast network of restaurants to deliver meals directly to customers' doorsteps via a fleet of delivery partners. Beyond food, Swiggy has expanded into a diversified hyperlocal service ecosystem that includes Instamart for quick grocery deliveries, Dineout for restaurant reservations, and other services aimed at providing urban consumers with unparalleled convenience.
